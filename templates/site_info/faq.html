FAQ


Who are the cooks?

Cooks are locals that love to cook, have spare seats and can offer you a meal.

Is it safe to enter their houses?

Cooks at canalcook.com are carefully selected from people we know and trust. We are expanding out network by referrals of these people, and all the kitchens are checked in person by our team before they become a part of our database. So yes, using canalcook.com is very safe.

Do you charge for your service?

We charge a small advance payment of three euros, as a way to confirm you reservation. At the kitchen, you will pay what you consume minus three euros.

How do I pay?

You pay by credit card, information is collected by Stripe. See stripe.com.
