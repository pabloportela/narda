FAQ


Who are the chefs?

Chefs are locals that love to cook, have spare seats and can offer you a meal.

Is it safe to enter their houses?

Chefs at Narda are carefully selected from people we know and trust. We are expanding out network by referrals of these people, and all the kitchens are checked in person by our team before they become a part of our database. So yes, using Narda is very safe.

Do you charge for your service?

We charge a small reservation fee for the service. But that is deducted from the price you pay in the kitchen. So what you see from the kitchen's menu is the final proice you pay. No surprises.

How do I pay?

You pay by credit card, information is collected by Stripe.

I have a voucher, how can I redeem it?

When selecting the payment method, click on the voucher option, enter your code, and press the "Book Meal" button.
