Why Narda

Paying a fortune for a ham and cheese sandwich? Wasting precious vacation time in a restaurant queue? Overspending just because you can't find alternatives? You are not the only one. Those are problems we've also faced and that's why we came up with Narda.

While we are struggling with that, in the same place, plenty of local people are having meals at their houses, willing to have us as guests. And what a great chance to jump out of the traditional tourist loop and get to know the real culture from the place you are in!

Eating well for a decent price is not that hard anymore. Start your Narda experience here.
