How it works

  • How to find a meal
  • How to offer your meals

  Find your meal

On Narda's homepage, make a search filtering by the desired date and place. Browse through the options, select one and book it.

  Log in if you haven't

If you haven't logged in yet, you will be asked to do so, with your Facebook account. We don't ask for much information, this is just to validate your identity.

  Book it

Once you book, you receive a confirmation email, with all the relevant information for your meal, including chef contact details. Then you can optionally get in contact with the chef.

  Enjoy it

Yeah, enjoy your meal, dude.

Just send us an email to chefs@haveachef.com and we will call you back. This is due to the extra care we want to have with our chefs to maintain the quality of our site.

For now we are offering the service only in Amsterdam, The Netherlands. More cities coming soon!
